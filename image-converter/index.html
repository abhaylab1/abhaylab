What's under the hood

Decoding leans on the browser's native image support plus a small library for TIFF; encoding for BMP and ICO is hand-rolled. Nothing here needs a server, an account, or a file size limit beyond your own RAM.

Writes out

.png.jpg.webp .bmp.ico.tiff

*AVIF decoding depends on your browser's built-in support. Animated GIFs and multi-page TIFFs convert from their first frame only.
